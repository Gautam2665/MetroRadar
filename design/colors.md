# Design System: Colors

MetroRadar follows a dark-first, premium color palette designed for high information density and professional diagnostics interfaces.

## Brand Colors

*   **Primary Background**: Matte Charcoal (`#09090b`)
*   **Card Background**: Deep Zinc (`#18181b`)
*   **Borders / Accents**: Muted Slate (`#27272a` / `#3f3f46`)
*   **Active selection**: MetroRadar Cyan (`#06b6d4`)
*   **Action Highlight**: MetroRadar Blue (`#3b82f6`)
*   **Text (Primary)**: Off-white (`#f4f4f5`)
*   **Text (Secondary)**: Muted Gray (`#a1a1aa` / `#71717a`)

## Status Colors

*   **Active / Healthy**: Emerald Green (`#10b981`)
*   **Construction / Warning**: Amber Yellow (`#f59e0b`)
*   **Suspended / Error**: Rose Red (`#f43f5e`)

## Operator Map Overlays

Transit lines will overlay onto the map using their official colors to maintain operator recognition:
-   **Delhi Yellow Line**: `#ffeb3b`
-   **Mumbai Line 2A**: `#ffd600`
-   **Mumbai Aqua Line 3**: `#00b0ff`
-   **Kochi Line**: `#4caf50`
